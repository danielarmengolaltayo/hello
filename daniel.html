My name is Daniel and I am here to participate in the conception and creation of alternatives to the current digital paradigm.

My hypothesis is that by better understanding how digital information operates and how we relate to it, we will be able to coordinate efforts more effectively towards common goals. I am focused on exploring and testing this idea to eventually transform it into something tangible that we can all benefit from.
