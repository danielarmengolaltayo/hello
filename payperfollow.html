Daniel Armengol Altayó
Pay-Per-Follow, 2018
Reperformance
This piece is a reperformance of Follow, a partnership between Kai Corvus and Daniel to bring the experience back, shifting the context to extend the conversation and ask new questions.
Pay one euro to control a person for one minute.

This is the deal announced by the performer responsible to seduce any passerby to try the experience.

Once someone is convinced to pay, and after this person controlled the movements of the other performer for 60 seconds, the first performer gives the money back while asking a question and offering three possible answers.

How much would you be willing to pay to have control over another person?

a) less than one euro
b) one euro
c) more than one euro
In the same way that some platforms temporarily take one euro from your bank account as a measure to confirm the validity of your payment card, the transaction happening in the performance pretends to direct the attention of the public and the tone for the subsequent conversation.
The unexpected reframing of the situation is designed to catch participants off guard, pushing them to quickly review their personal scale of values, in order to understand and balance the ethical implications of a past decision with the economic commitment of a future decision.

At what price would you let others control you?
